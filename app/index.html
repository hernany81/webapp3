Welcome to
emote-app

emote-app provides you a way to share reviews in short form (called emotes) for any "title" you choose.

Example:

title: 'Iron Man 3'

emotes: 'thrilling film'; 'must watch'; 'exciting plot'

Click this create button to start creating your emotes.

The emote input area is very versatile, it lets you type an emote and converts it into a 'tag' format when you press comma or enter.When the tag is ready it defaults to a yellow colour (which is used as a positive emote). You can change this colour to grey by clicking on it (grey means negative emote).

You can share your emotes to facebook using the share button.

The posts on the feed page show you recent posts by emote community.

If you click on the small user pictures, you are shown emotes from that particular user.

Clicking on someone else's emote creates a re-emote from your profile - as if you had said exactly the same thing.

You can swipe sideways inside each post to see a summary of the popular emotes for that particular title.

The 'Doing Now' button is useful to let people know that you are currently enjoying a great meal or a movie, it remains active for an hour.

You can view your favourites from the menu (top left button).

All the users which are 'doing' this title related experience right now will have a green activity bulb on their profile photo.

Actions like favourites, todo lists, add photo, share and report are available right there on each post.
